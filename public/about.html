About Me

Welcome to the about page of a tech enthusiast whose journey began in the halls of Heritage Convent, where he pursued his HSLC education. From those early days, his fascination with technology was evident, spending countless hours exploring the inner workings of computers and dreaming up innovative solutions.

During his time at Heritage Convent, he discovered his passion for building tech stuff, tinkering with hardware, and dabbling in programming languages. It was here that he laid the foundation for his future endeavors.

Upon completing his HSLC, he ventured into the world of COHSEM at MECI Explorer Academy. Here, he honed his skills in programming, particularly in languages like C and C++, and embarked on various small projects, each one fueling his curiosity and ambition further.

Eager to deepen his understanding and broaden his horizons, he set his sights on pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at Royal Global University. This marked a significant milestone in his journey towards becoming a full-stack developer.

Throughout his academic pursuits, he remains steadfast in his aspiration to become a full-stack developer, equipped with the knowledge and skills to tackle challenges across the entire spectrum of software development. With each step forward, he embraces new technologies, hones his craft, and strives to make a meaningful impact in the world of technology.
